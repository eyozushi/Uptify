Privacy Policy for Uptify

Last updated: December 10, 2024

1. Data Collection

Uptify does not collect, store, or share any personal data with external servers. All your data is stored locally on your device.

2. Camera and Photo Access

Uptify requests camera and photo library access only to allow you to set your ideal self image. These images are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted elsewhere.

3. Notifications

Uptify uses local notifications to remind you of tasks and habits. No notification data is sent to external servers.

4. Data Storage

All task data, settings, and images are stored locally using your device's secure storage. We do not have access to this data.

5. Third-Party Services

Uptify does not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising services.

6. Children's Privacy

Uptify does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: uptify.app@gmail.com
